# Ceftriaxone

## Ceftriaxone Kabi inj 1gm

*藥物代碼*：ICTX

*給藥途徑*：I

*用法用量*：

Adult & children >12 years 1-2 g IV infusion once daily. 
Severe infection: 2-4 g once daily. 
Infant & children 20-80 mg/kg/day; neonate 20-50 mg/kg/day.

*****NEONATE ANTIBIOTICS DOSE*****
