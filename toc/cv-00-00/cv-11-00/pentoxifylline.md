# Pentoxifylline

## Ceretal S.C. 400mg

##### 

| Title              | Content                                                                      |
|:-------------------|:-----------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| TAH Drug Code      | OCER4                                                                        |
| Indications        | Peripheral circulatory disorders， chronic occlusive arterial disease.       |
| Dosing             | 1 tab TID with meal.                                                         |
| Hepatic Impairment | Dose adjustment not necessary                                                |
