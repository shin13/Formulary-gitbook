# Flunarizine

## Suzin 5mg

##### 

| 標題       | 說明                                                                                                                                                                      |
|:-----------|:--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| 藥品代碼   | OSUZ                                                                                                                                                                      |
| 適應症     | Prophylaxis of migraine, maintenance treatment of cerebral circulation disorders.                                                                                         |
| 用法用量   | 10 mg bid for 4 weeks.                                                                                                                                                    |
| 肝功能異常 | 無需調整劑量                                                                                                                                                              |
