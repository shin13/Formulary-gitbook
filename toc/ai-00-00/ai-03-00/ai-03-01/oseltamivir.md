# Oseltamivir

## Eraflu 75mg (Government)

##### 

| 藥品代碼   | OERAF                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        |
|:-----------|:---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| 適應症     | Prophylaxis & treatment of influenza in adult & children.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    |
| 用法用量   | (仿單) Influenza: Treatment (for patients > 1 year): - Adult: Usual Dose for uncomplicated influenza of <48 hrs duration: 75 mg po bid x 5 days; complicated influenza in immunocompromised patients even if ill over 48 hrs: 75 mg po bid x 10 or more days. - Children > 40 kg: 75 mg bid x 5 days - Children > 23-40 kg: 60 mg bid x 5 days - Children > 15-23 kg: 45 mg bid x 5 days - Children ? 15 kg: 30 mg bid x 5 days - Infant 0-12 months: 3 mg/kg bid x 5 days Prophylaxis (for patients > 1 year): - Adults: 75 mg orally once daily for 10 days to 6 weeks. - Children > 40 kg: 75 mg qd x 10 days - Children > 23-40 kg: 60 mg qd x 10 days - Children > 15-23 kg: 45 mg qd x 10 days - Children ? 15 kg: 30 mg qd x 10 days - Infants 3-11 months: 3 mg/kg once daily (熱病) |
| 肝功能異常 | 無需調整劑量                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 |
| 腎功能異常 | 需調整劑量                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   |
| 禁忌       | Hypersensitivity.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            |
| 副作用     | Nausea， vomiting， abdominal pain， headache. Insomnia， diarrhea. Gastrointestinal: Abdominal pain (2% to 5% )， Nausea (4% to 10% )， Vomiting (2% to 15% )                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               |
| 孕期建議   | Compatible – maternal benefit >> embryo-fetal risk                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           |
| 哺乳期建議 | Compatible 哺乳時可使用                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      |
