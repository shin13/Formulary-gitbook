# Tigecycline

## Tygacil inj 50mg

##### 臨採

| 藥品代碼   | ITYG                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   |
|:-----------|:---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| 適應症     | Treatment of complicated skin & skin structure infections， complicated intra-abdominal infections & community-acquired pneumonia caused by susceptible microorganisms sensitive to tigecycline.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       |
