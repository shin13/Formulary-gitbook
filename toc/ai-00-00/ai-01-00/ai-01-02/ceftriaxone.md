# Ceftriaxone

## Ceftriaxone Kabi inj 1gm

_藥物代碼_：ICTX

_給藥途徑_：I

_用法用量_：

Adult & children &gt;12 years 1-2 g IV infusion once daily. Severe infection: 2-4 g once daily. Infant & children 20-80 mg/kg/day; neonate 20-50 mg/kg/day.
