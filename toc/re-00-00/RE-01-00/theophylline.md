# Theophylline

## Centertheo 60mL (5.34mg-mL) liquid

*藥物代碼*：LCEN

*給藥途徑*：O

*用法用量*：

Theophylline 5.34mg/ml

Adult 200-250 mg 
Children 6-12 years 125-250 mg. 
To be taken every 6 hr.
