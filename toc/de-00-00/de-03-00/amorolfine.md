| 副作用             | In exceptional cases, a slight, transient periungual burning sensation was observed after the application of the nail lacquer.                                                                                                                                |
| 禁忌               | Patients who have shown hypersensitivity to any ingredient of Loceryl.                                                                                                                                                                                        |
