# Amorolfine

## Amocoat Nail Lacquer 5mL

*藥物代碼*：EAMO

*給藥途徑*：E

*用法用量*：

Adult: Topical Fungal nail infections As 5% nail lacquer: Apply 1-2 times/wk. Usual treatment duration: 6 mth for fingernails and 9-12 mth for toenails.
