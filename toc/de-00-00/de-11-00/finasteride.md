# Finasteride

| 藥品代碼       | OPROP                                                                                                                                                               |
|:---------------|:--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| 適應症         | Treatment of male pattern hair loss (androgenetic alopecia) in men only. Not indicated in women & children.                                                         |
| 用法用量       | 1 mg once daily.                                                                                                                                                    |
