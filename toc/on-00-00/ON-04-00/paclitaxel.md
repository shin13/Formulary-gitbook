# Paclitaxel

## FORMOXOL inj 60mg-10mL

*藥物代碼*：IFOR10

*給藥途徑*：I

*用法用量*：

135-175 mg/m2 IV over 3 hr every 3 weeks. Patients must be premedicated dexamethasone, diphenhydramine & cimetidine prior to administration.
