# Degarelix

## FIRMAGON inj 120mg-3mL

*藥物代碼*：IFIR

*給藥途徑*：I

*用法用量*：

Initially 240 mg as 2 consecutive SC inj of 120 mg each. Maintenance: 80 mg as SC inj monthly. Should be given 1 month after starting dose.
