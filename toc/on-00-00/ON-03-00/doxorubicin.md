# Doxorubicin

## Lipo-Dox inj 20mg(自費用)

*藥物代碼*：ILIPD

*給藥途徑*：I

*用法用量*：

Breast/Ovarian cancer 50 mg/m2 by IV infusion once every 4 weeks. AIDS-related Kaposi's sarcoma 20 mg/m2 by IV infusion every 2-3 weeks.

## Lipo-Dox inj 20mg (健保用)

*藥物代碼*：ILIPD1

*給藥途徑*：I

*用法用量*：

Breast/Ovarian cancer 50 mg/m2 by IV infusion once every 4 weeks. AIDS-related Kaposi's sarcoma 20 mg/m2 by IV infusion every 2-3 weeks.
