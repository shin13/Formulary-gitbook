# Pemetrexed

## Alimta inj 100mg

*藥物代碼*：IALI1

*給藥途徑*：I

*用法用量*：

500 mg/m2 by IV infusion over 10 mins on Day 1 of each 21-day cycle. In malignant mesothelioma, cisplatin (75 mg/m2) is given 30 mins after the end of pemetrexed infusion.
