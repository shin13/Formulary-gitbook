| 藥品代碼       | OBRO                                                                                                             |
|:---------------|:-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| 適應症         | Inflammation after operation & traumatic injury， inadequate expectoration of sputum in respiratory disease.     |
| 用法用量       | 3-6 tablets/day in divided doses 6-8 hrly.                                                                       |
