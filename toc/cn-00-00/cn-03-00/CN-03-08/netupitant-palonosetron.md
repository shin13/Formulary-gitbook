# Netupitant + Palonosetron

## Akynzeo 300-0.5mg

*藥物代碼*：OAKY

*給藥途徑*：O

*用法用量*：

Highly emetogenic chemotherapy (including cisplatin-based regimens): Administer netupitant 300 mg/palonosetron 0.5 mg (1 capsule) orally 1 hour prior to chemotherapy on day 1; coadminister dexamethasone 12 mg orally 30 minutes prior to chemotherapy on day 1, and 8 mg orally on days 2 to 4.

Anthracycline and cyclophosphamide-based regimens and other regimens not highly emetogenic: Administer netupitant 300 mg/palonosetron 0.5 mg (1 capsule) orally 1 hour prior to chemotherapy on day 1; coadminister dexamethasone 12 mg orally 30 minutes before chemotherapy on day 1.
