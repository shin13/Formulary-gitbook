# Risperidone

## Risperdal solution 1mg-mL, 30mL

*藥物代碼*：LRIS

*給藥途徑*：O

*用法用量*：

Adult May be given once or bid. Initially 2 mg/day. Usual dose: 2-6 mg/day. Elderly or patient with renal or liver disease Initially 0.5 mg bid, increasing to 1-2 mg bid.
