|:-----------|:------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| 藥品代碼   | ISOLM                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   |

| 用法用量   | For large dose, IV 30mg/kg for 10-20 minutes, could repeat every 4-6 hours in 48 hours. In other indication, 10-40mg for the first dose, adjusted by patient's condition from second dose.
