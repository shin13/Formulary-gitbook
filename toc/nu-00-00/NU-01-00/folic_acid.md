# Folic Acid

## Folic acid 5mg

*藥物代碼*：OFA

*給藥途徑*：O

*用法用量*：

Adult Initially 1 TAB tid for 14 days. Maintenance: 1 TAB daily for 1-7 days. Children 6-12 years 2 TAB/day, 1-5 years 1 TAB/day, <1 years 0.5 mg/kg body weight/day.
