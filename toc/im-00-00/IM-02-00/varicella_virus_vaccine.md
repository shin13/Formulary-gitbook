# Varicella Virus Vaccine

## Varivax Vaccine 0.5mL-dose (Private)

*藥物代碼*：IVAR

*給藥途徑*：I

*用法用量*：

By SC inj. Adult & children >=13 years 2 doses with 4-8 weeks interval. Children 12 months-12 years 1 dose.
