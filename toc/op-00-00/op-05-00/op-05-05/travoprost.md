# Travoprost

| Indications        | Reduction of intraocular pressure in patients with open-angle glaucoma or ocular HTN.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    |
| Dosing             | Instill 1 drop in the affected eye(s) once every evening.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                |
