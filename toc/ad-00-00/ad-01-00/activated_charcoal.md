# Activated Charcoal

## Carbomix 61.5gm

#### 一般可用

| 藥品代碼       | OCAR                                                                                                                                                                                                                    |
|:---------------|:------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| 適應症         | 為活性碳，解毒之用(B0215491CC) Emergency treatment of acute oral poisoning or drug overdose. Also binding of bacteriotoxins & other diarrhea-inducing substances in the GIT.                                            |

| 腎功能異常     | 無需調整劑量                                                                                                                                                                                                            |
